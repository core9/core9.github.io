Part I. Overview of Core9 Framework

The Core9 Framework is not your average framework. Meaning in general frameworks provide some kind of functionality usually in the application layer. Core9 doesn't provide any functionality it's main goal is to provide good standards and ideas to grow code. So instead of focusing on code that should make your life easier Core9 focuses on the methods and ideas that allow you to create testable, and low maintenance quality code yourself.

The reason why core9 was created was the observation that most frameworks promise you everything you want such as code quality, unambiguous coding standards and testability. However in the hands of the average developer all those promises quickly disappear for whatever reason time, budget etc..

Core9 was created from the idea that bad things are unavoidable and that in addressing this reality there might be a solution to creating that much desired deliverable many call "Quality Software".

Some of the assumptions that were made creating Core9 was that the biggest corrupter of good software is incidental complexity due to whatever reason. The way that Core9 tries to deal with this problem is to provide a method of coding that focuses the developer to note some of the main ideas that were produced in the short history of programming.

So rather than being a bag of goodies Core9 tries to be a book of experiences and ideas as we recognize a developers most important tool is his intellectual capacity and his only true framework is that of his own mind.
